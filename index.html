About Ignis

In the early 2030s, the world was at a breaking point. Electric vehicles had become the norm, but the environmental cost of lithium mining and battery waste was devastating. Landfills filled with toxic, non-biodegradable batteries, and the energy sector faced a crisis—how could we power the future without destroying the planet?

That’s when a team of visionary engineers, chemists, and environmentalists came together in London. Their mission: to create a battery that was powerful, sustainable, and adaptable—one that could fuel both electric and combustion vehicles while leaving zero harmful waste behind.

After years of relentless innovation, Ignis was born.
